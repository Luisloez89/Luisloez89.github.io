PhoMod3D

The photogrammetric dream of modeling the 3D world at scale only from photographs becomes reality.

Photogrammetry at your fingertips

Phomod3D was designed as the tool that will make sharing 3D world an activity of great importance for users of Windows Phone platform. The application was provided with a capture assistant and allows the full management of your projects.

Windows phone

Designed according of Windows Phone 8.1 directives, PhoMod3D offers a simple and intuitive interface. The tool has been awarded by Microsoft AppCampus fulfilling its quality standards.
