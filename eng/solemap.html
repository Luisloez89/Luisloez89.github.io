SolEMap

Solar Energy Mapping

Software for the automatic detection of solar panels suitable location from 3D surface models of roofs with thermographic information.

Facilities for "Smart Cities"

SolEMAP has been developed in order to provide to the user an automatic tool for assessing large areas covered, estimating the most optimal zones for the installation of solar panels. The geometric evaluation of the roofs is complemented with thermographic information and climate information (over a defined period of time), thus allowing the detection of obstacles and the estimation of incident solar radiation on each roof surface.

Input data

The input is a 3D model of the roof surfaces with thermographic information.

Final product

The final result is a georeferenced 3D model classified according to its solar potential. Additionally, the software provides the exact coordinates of the optimum locations for installing solar panels, its surface and the estimated solar irradiation according to historical climatological data from the nearest station.
